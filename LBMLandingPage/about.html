About

Built to help local businesses grow.

LB Marketing was founded on the belief that local businesses deserve a marketing partner who proves their value every month.

Anthony Omar Valenzuela

Founder · LB Marketing

A quick introduction.

I'm Anthony Omar Valenzuela, the founder of LB Marketing. I graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science, and I bring 5+ years of experience in IT services to this business.

I started LB Marketing for a simple reason: to help smaller local businesses reach a bigger audience and generate more traffic. Local businesses are the backbone of every community, but most of them are competing in a digital landscape that wasn't built for them. Bigger companies have the budget and the in-house teams; smaller businesses are left trying to figure it out on their own, or paying retainers to agencies that don't actually deliver.

My goal is to change that. By combining hands-on local SEO and reputation work with the technical and analytical background I bring from my IT services experience, I'm able to deliver results that are normally only accessible to businesses with much larger budgets — and do it transparently, month-to-month, with no lock-in.

If you run a local business and you're looking for a marketing partner who'll show up, do the work, and prove it with real numbers every month, I'd love to talk.

What we stand for

Four principles that guide every engagement.

These aren't slogans — they're rules I built the business around because they're the things most agencies get wrong.

01

Results before retention

Every engagement starts with a 30-day sprint designed to produce something you can point to — not a promise of future results.

02

No lock-in, ever

Month-to-month engagements. If you're not seeing value, you leave. That pressure keeps us sharp and honest.

03

Built for small business

This business exists to help smaller local businesses reach a bigger audience — not to chase large enterprise contracts.

04

Transparency by default

Every report shows exactly what we did and what it produced. No marketing jargon, just before-and-after numbers.
