Month 1 Sprint

The first month is about reviews and visibility.

Our entire goal in Month 1 is to grow your Google review count and lay the foundation for sustained local search visibility. Real reviews from real customers is the single biggest local ranking signal — so that's where we focus first.

Important: Not a guarantee

Review growth depends on your customer volume, your customers' willingness to leave reviews, and Google's evolving guidelines. We can't promise a specific number — but we can promise to run a compliant, professional campaign and report exactly what it produced.

Days 1–5

Digital Presence Audit

Complete written audit of your Google Business Profile, website, existing reviews, competitor positioning, and where you currently rank for your top keywords.

Week 2

GBP Optimization

Every field on your Google Business Profile gets completed and optimized — categories, services, photos, hours, descriptions. Existing reviews are responded to.

Week 3

Review Campaign Launch

Personalized review requests sent to recent customers via email. The goal is meaningful review growth — though final volume depends on customer engagement.

Week 4

Before/After Report

A written report showing review count change, rating movement, profile views, ranking shifts, and the priority plan for Month 2 onward.
